Overview

Programmable power distribution and load control module planned as the third part of the AILUOHAI integrated onboard electronics system.

Use this page to explain what problems the future PDM solves: cleaner harnesses, centralised switching, diagnostics and better serviceability in non-standard vessel builds.

Key functions

  • Electronic switching for loads without conventional fuse panel logic.
  • Current monitoring and overload response.
  • Coordination with ECU and dashboard alerts.
  • Reduction of wiring complexity in custom vessels.
  • Future-ready module for unified onboard control architecture.

Application

Designed for airboats and vessels with non-standard operating conditions, including moisture exposure, vibration and integration of multiple onboard electronic modules.
